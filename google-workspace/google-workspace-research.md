## Options Researched

### Option 1 - Free Gmail Accounts
- Cost: Free immediately
- Email format: @gmail.com
- No admin control panel
- Quick to set up
- Less professional appearance

### Option 2 - Google Workspace for Nonprofits (Recommended long term)
- Cost: Free with nonprofit approval
- Email format: @yourdomain.org
- Full admin control panel
- Managed shared drives
- Professional appearance
- Requires Companies House registration number
- Takes several days for approval

### Option 3 - Google Workspace Paid
- Cost: £5 per user per month (£300/year for 5 trustees)
- Same features as Nonprofit plan
- Not recommended as NGN qualifies for free nonprofit plan

---

## Recommendation

**Phase 1 (Immediate):** Create free Gmail accounts for all 5 trustees
**Phase 2 (Long term):** Apply for Google Workspace for Nonprofits at google.com/nonprofits
