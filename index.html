About Nottingham Growing Network

Nottingham Growing Network is a long-standing initiative that builds links between 35 community food growing groups in Nottingham, with a focus on healthy eating, wellbeing, sustainable gardening and biodiversity.

Our community gardens teach people how to grow food and cook with fresh ingredients. Food grown is shared with volunteers, local food banks, and the wider community.
