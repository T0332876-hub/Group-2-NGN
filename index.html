About Nottingham Growing Network

Nottingham Growing Network builds links between 35 community food growing groups across Nottingham, focusing on healthy eating, wellbeing, sustainable gardening and biodiversity.

Our gardens grow food together and share it with volunteers, local food banks, and the wider community. Each garden is unique — find yours and get involved.
